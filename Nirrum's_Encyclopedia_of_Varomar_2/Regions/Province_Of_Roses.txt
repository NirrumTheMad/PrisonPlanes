====== Province Of Roses ======
Created Saturday 28 October 2023

The province of roses is a temperate region stretching from BIG BAY to the edge of the aegis mountains to the northeast, near the yetsin river basin. It is bordered to the East by Carper's sea, and to the West by the Aegis mountains
