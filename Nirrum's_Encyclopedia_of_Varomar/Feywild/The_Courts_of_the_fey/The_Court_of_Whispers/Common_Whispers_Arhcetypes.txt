====== Common Whispers Arhcetypes ======
Created Tuesday 21 November 2023


The most common forms of whispers fey 

**Keysprites **which steal small items around the house and move them to areas which are roughly calculated to manipulate how long it takes to find them
**Foddlers, **sneaky beings that wear out items faster
**Meenhu **Lead children astray on dark woodland paths
**Dream Spirits **Deliver specially made dreams to the minds of the sleeping.
