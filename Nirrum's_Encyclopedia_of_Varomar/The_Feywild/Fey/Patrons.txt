====== Patrons ======
Created Sunday 23 August 2020

All fey Patrons have the following abilities along with their own abilities

Free spells
Eldritch blast, Freedom of Movement, Invisibility, silent image, alter self, Disguise self, Conjure fey, Conjure woodland critters

Low Spells (10 slots, recharging on a short rest)
Faerie fire, Sleep, Calm emotions, Phantasmal force, blink, plant growth,  Dominate beast, greater invisibility, dominate person, seeming, Confusion, Modify memory

Arcanum Spells (5 slots, recharging on a short rest)
	Bestow Curse, slow, shapechange, Compulsion, weird, true polymorph,imprisonment, feeblmind, Dominate monster, Mental PRison, Mass suggestion, flesh to stone
	
Fey presence,
	All creatures within a 60ft cube of the patron must succeed on a wisdom saving throw against their influence DC, creatures that fail their saving throws are frightened or charmed by the patron (their choice) for 1 minute, an affected creature may make the save again at the end of each of their turns. 

Misty Movement
	instead of moving, the patron may teleport up to 60ft and may remain invisible until the start of their next turn or until they cast a spell
	
Immunity to the game
	Patrons are immune to being charmed and when a creature attempts to charm them, they can use their reaction to attempt to turn the charm back on that creature. The creature must make a wisdom saving throw against the patron's influence DC or be charmed for 1 minute
	
Dark Delerium
	the patron can plunge a creature into an illusory realm. As an action, choose a creature that the patron can see within 60 feet of it  must make a Wisdom saving throw against their influence DC. On a failed save, it is charmed or frightened by the patron (your choice) for 1 minute or until their concentration is broken (as if you are concentrating on a spell). This effect ends early if the creature takes any damage.
	
	Until this illusion ends, the creature thinks it is lost in a misty realm, the appearance of which the patron chooses. The creature can see and hear only itself, the patron, and the illusion.
	
	The Patron must finish a short or long rest before you can use this feature again.
	
Truesight 30ft
The ability to speak with animals and plants
