====== The Shadow ======
Created Saturday 19 August 2023

The Raven Queen, the anapotheotic lady, Amnesis. The story of this one is one of the greatest tragedies, written in deific blood by the quill of celestial hubris. She cannot remember who she was, she does not know where the rest of her is. She spits curses upon the gods and depises pride beyond all things. Also too does she hate the undead, as part of her feud with orcus. She appears usually as a beautiful elven queen, when she can appear at all
