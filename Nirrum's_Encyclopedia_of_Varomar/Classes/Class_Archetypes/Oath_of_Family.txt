====== Oath of Family ======
Created Monday 13 April 2020

Your oath goes something like this:
	**Family first, Everything else is expendable.**
		Take action to benefit and save the family. It might be best to think through your actions first, otherwise you'll endanger them again.
	**Represent your Family.**
		Your actions don't just belong to you but to your clan. Your fame is theirs, Your infamy as well. 
	**Make your choices carefully**
		Especially if you're making the family bigger, your choices in those you associate with are also associated with the family.
